Project Requirements & Analysis
1. User Roles (Identity Framework)
Admin: Full access, manage employees, departments, designations.
Manager: Approve/Reject leave requests, view team attendance.
Employee: Mark attendance (In/Out), apply for leave, view history.
2. Functional Modules
Authentication: Login, Register, Logout (using ASP.NET Core Identity).
Organization: Manage Departments and Designations.
Employee Management: Employee profiles linked to Identity Users.
Attendance:
Clock In / Clock Out.
Prevent double clock-in.
Calculate working hours.
Leave Management:
Leave Types (Sick, Casual, Annual).
Request Leave.
Workflow: Pending -> Approved/Rejected.
3. Architecture (N-Layer)
Domain Layer: Entities and Enums.
Data Layer: DbContext, Migrations, Repositories.
Service Layer: Business Logic (Interfaces & Implementation).
Presentation Layer: MVC Controllers and Views.
